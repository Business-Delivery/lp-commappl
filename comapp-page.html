Delivery and Shipping Details

Expanded delivery is available in select metropolitan areas. In those areas, orders of less than $100, before tax, will be charged a $10 delivery surcharge. Delivery to the expanded zone is available within three business days of ordering, and is provided by a small package delivery provider. Due to shipping restrictions, not all products are offered for delivery in the expanded zone.The delivery area is defined by ZIP codes. To determine if your ZIP code is eligible for delivery, click "Set Delivery ZIP code" in the top left above the Costco Business Center logo.

Delivery Requirements

The following requirements apply to receive delivery of your order from UPS:

  • A valid Costco Membership is required.

  • A physical delivery address must be provided. We cannot deliver to PO Boxes.

Delivery Hours & Lead Time

Packages are delivered Monday through Friday during normal business hours. Deliveries may arrive as soon as one to two days, when you place your order online by 3 p.m. Orders placed after 3 p.m. will be processed the following business day, for delivery within two to three business days. For additional information on delivery and shipping service and fees, visit Delivery and Shipping Details on our Customer Service page.
